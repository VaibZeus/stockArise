Financial projection report

An FPR in simple words is a PDF file of generally 12-15 pages in which a startup's future financial health is shown. such as next 5 years of Profit and loss statement, startup budget, financial ratios such as return on equity, return on capital employed, Free cash flows and other important KPIs along with graphs depicting these vital financial numbers.

Pith deck

Essentially, a pitch-deck is a PowerPoint presentation document that represents your startup ideas while you are presenting your company. It must be original, well-researched, attractive, and educational, but it shouldn't sound overly legalistic. the ability to impress investors will depend on the startup’s pitch deck and its presentation

Business plan

If the funding amount you are expecting to get is over ₹5 Crores. StockArise experts would advice to get a business plan. Investors want to have faith that their money will be well spent and they expect more returns than any other Investment area. You'll use your business plan as a tool to persuade individuals that working with you or investing in your business such a large chunk of capital is a wise move
